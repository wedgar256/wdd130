My Favorite City

Kampala

Kampala is the capital city of Uganda. It is located in the central region of the country and is known for its vibrant culture, bustling markets, and beautiful landscapes. The city is home to many historical sites, including the Kasubi Tombs, which are a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Kampala also has a lively nightlife scene and is a hub for music and arts in Uganda.

I would love to visit Kampala because of the hospitality, the food and the culture.
