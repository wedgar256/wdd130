Uganda

Uganda is a landlocked East African country bordered by Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, DRC, and South Sudan, with Kampala as its capital. It's renowned for its diverse landscapes (including Lake Victoria, the Nile's source, and the Rwenzori Mountains), rich wildlife (gorillas, safaris in parks like Queen Elizabeth and Bwindi), and cultural diversity (over 50 tribes, English as the official language). The economy relies on agriculture (coffee, tea), with growing tech sectors, though it faces challenges like poverty. Known as the "Pearl of Africa," it has a tropical climate and a history of resilience since independence in 1962.
